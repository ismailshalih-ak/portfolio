Core Aesthetic: "The Architectural Code"
Palette:
Background: Off-white / Cream #F4F3EF (Warm, paper-like texture).
Primary Text: Jet Black #111111.
Accents:
Electric Indigo: #4F46E5 (Headings/Highlights).
Safety Orange: #FF5722 (Interactive states/Buttons/Hover).
Typography:
Display Font (Headings): Space Grotesk or Archivo Black. Thick, geometric, impactful.
Body Font: Space Mono or JetBrains Mono. Monospace fonts convey "code" without being cliché.
Visual Language:
Thick (3px) black borders on all cards and containers.
Sharp corners (0px border-radius) or extremely subtle rounding (4px).
Hard drop shadows (no blur): box-shadow: 6px 6px 0px #000000.
Use of horizontal rules (<hr>) as major design dividers.
